Lasagna

Taking lasagne to next level

Is lasagne already your favorite food, but still wondering if it could be better? Look no further because I am going to discover my family's secret lasagne recipe!

First off you need excellent ingredients. On top of ingredients you need to follow instructions precisely.

ingredients

  • Lasagne levyt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Buy ingredients
  2. Cook minced meat
  3. Throw all ingredients to tray

  5. Voila!
